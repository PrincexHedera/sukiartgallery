The Gallery

Suki's Art Gallery is a showcase of feline creativity in a serene and imaginative setting. Suki's paw-painted masterpieces capture the essence of her artistic spirit, and her portfolio is always evolving to reflect her latest inspirations.

Art Commissions

Suki is currently not accepting commissions, as she prefers to lay on windowsills and yell at her roommates. However, if you happen to bribe her with a can of wet food, Suki's talents are at your service.

Exhibition

Discover Suki's world through her captivating exhibitions. From playful strokes to bold brushwork, each collection tells a different story. Visit our gallery to immerse yourself in the captivating world of feline artistry.

Location and Visiting Hours

Suki's Art Gallery is located in the Rust Belt Market in Ferndale, where local, independent vendors sell their crafts. We welcome visitors with open paws!

Tuesday through Saturday: 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM
Sunday: 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM
